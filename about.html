About Me, Michael Thompson

I am an amatuer artist and professional urban planner living and working in New York City. The City--and Places around the world--are my inspiration for my sketching and painting. I am most interested in storytelling about a place through interesting visual features and color.

If you're interested in purchasing any of the prints you see here, please visit my etsy page. For custom paintings, commissions, and original work, please inquire via email at m.thomps329@gmail.com.

Custom Artwork

If there is a place special to you, I do custom artwork upon request. Like all other work on this site, the custom paintings are made with professional caliber materials. Typically, sizes for custom work are 5"x7", 8"x11", and 12"x16". For more information, please contact me via email at m.thomps329@gmail.com.
