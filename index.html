Manhattan Bridge

Brooklyn, New York

The Manhattan Bridge from Washington Street is an iconic, must-visit area for tourists and local New Yorkers alike. This neighborhood, known as Down Under the Manhattan Bridge Overpass (DUMBO), sits directly across from Lower Manhattan. The painting shows former industrial buildings and cobblestone streets that frame the Manhattan Bridge and Empire State Building below

Stone Cabin

Margate, England

This historic stone cabin was part of a social media fueled art challenge, which the artist happily accepted. The picture resulted in a quintessential vacation cabin along the mountains waterfront in the British Isles. Framed by the ocean on the right and a mountain on the left, the cabin and its features are contrasted with the yellow, red, and orange objects at the base. The doorway is framed by the sturdy rock as the staircase leads occupants into the cozy dwelling.

Galway Bay Boats

Galway, Ireland

This riparian scene shows the docked boats in Galway Bay near the Claddagh neighborhood of Galway, Ireland. Long known as one of western Ireland's most historic cities, Galway has a long fishing and boating history thanks to its generously-flowing waterways and accessible bay. Many locals use this area of Galway Bay to dock their fishing, recreational, and working boats.

Istanbul Cafes

Istanbul, Turkey

The Istanbul Cafes painting shows the lighter building colors and abundance of street trees typically found around Istanbul. As the confluence of Europe and Asia, Istanbul has a storied history as a meeting place and a center of trade, both of which continue to this day. The yellow and brown buildings frame the newer, white apartment building in the middle, which is juxtaposed with the cobblestone streets that show the cities' original transportation material.
